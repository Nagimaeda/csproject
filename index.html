What is SOGIE?

SOGIE stands for Sexual Orientation and Gender Identity/Expression.

Sexual Orientation - Refers to the gender of the people one feels sexual or emotional attraction
for the same gender, opposite gender, both genders, all genders, and more

Gender Identity - Refers to how one identifies as; man, woman, neither, fluid, etc. A biological
male can identify as a woman. A biological woman can identify as a man.

Gender Expression - Refers to how one expresses their gender identity usually appearance, clothing
and behavior; most common classifications are masculine and feminine expression.
