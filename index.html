What is SOGIE?

SOGIE stands for Sexual Orientation and Gender Identity/Expression.

Sexual Orientation - Refers to the gender of the people one feels sexual or emotional attraction
for the same gender, opposite gender, both genders, all genders, and more

Gender Identity - Refers to how one identifies as; man, woman, neither, fluid, etc. A biological
male can identify as a woman. A biological woman can identify as a man.

Gender Expression - Refers to how one expresses their gender identity usually appearance, clothing
and behavior; most common classifications are masculine and feminine expression.

Changes After Implementation

After the implementation of the SOGIE Bill, people afraid of expressing their SOGIE might feel safer as
society will hopefully become more open-minded and knowledgable about the said topic thanks to
the programs to be launched by the government. The summary of this bill, as we see it, is that people
who belong to the LGBTQI+ will get back their rights that are being tarnished by the unlawful
discriminatory practices implemented in our current society.

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Protects the human rights of people included in LGBTQI+
  • Lessens discrimination by fining violators
  • Aims to educate people about SOGIE
  • May provide more opportunities for the LGBTQI+ community as they'll be less discriminated
  • SOGIE-related concerns or cases will be attended to more.

Cons

  • Raises questions about biological gender specific jobs (gynecologists, etc.)
  • Programs to educate the masses about SOGIE are likely to fail.
  • Corrupt officials can let the offenses pass due to connections.
  • SOGIE is against all-around discrimination, but bill only implies gender discrimination.
