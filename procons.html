Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Protects the human rights of people included in LGBTQI+
  • Lessens discrimination by fining violators
  • Aims to educate people about SOGIE
  • May provide more opportunities for the LGBTQI+ community as they'll be less discriminated
  • SOGIE-related concerns or cases will be attended to more.

Cons

  • Raises questions about biological gender specific jobs (gynecologists, etc.)
  • Programs to educate the masses about SOGIE are likely to fail.
  • Corrupt officials can let the offenses pass due to connections.
  • SOGIE is against all-around discrimination, but bill only implies gender discrimination.
